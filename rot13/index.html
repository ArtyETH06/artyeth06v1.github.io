GBQB yvfgr :
- Cnva (2 onthrggrf)
- Ynvg (1 yvger)
- Pbevnaqer (fhegbhg cnf, p'rfg cnf oba)
- 4 onanarf, 4 cbzzrf, 4 benatrf
- Cbhyrg (4 svyrgf qr cbhyrg)
- 1 synt : SPFP{rq24p7sq86p2s0515366}
- Câgrf (1xt)
- Evm (fnp qr 18xt)
- Abheve zba qvabfnher
